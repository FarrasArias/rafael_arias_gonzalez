About Me

With a Master of Science in Interactive Arts and Technology from Simon Fraser University and over a decade of experience across academia and industry, I craft compelling interactive experiences with engaging narratives. I've co-founded gaming startups, taught thousands of students, and spoken at international conferences on generative AI, game development, and audio technology. Currently, I'm passionate about sharing my knowledge as an instructor at both the Vancouver Film School (VFS) and Simon Fraser University (SFU).



Beyond the technical aspects, I truly believe in responsible development, emphasizing empathy and humanity in everything I create and teach. It's about building experiences that not only captivate but also resonate positively with people.
